The Kitchen

A knife-wielding chef and every cuisine he respects.

Father Fats has been on Clark Street since 2014. The concept is simple: a knife-wielding masterful Chef putting together the ultimate hit list of ingredients, sourced from local Wisconsin farms and filtered through just about every cuisine he respects.

The result tastes like New York, Asia, The Deep South, and Spain all sharing a table in Stevens Point. No reservations. No fixed menu. Mix and match, taste and graze. Featured on PBS Wisconsin's Main Streets in March 2026 alongside Chef's Kitchen next door.
